## Project Overview

This project analyses the sales performance of a cosmetic firm to gain insight into sales trends, product performance, and customer behaviour. 

## Project Objectives

- Develop a centralised data repository from all business locations to ensure data consistency and accuracy
- Conduct Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on sales performance
- Develop a user-friendly dashboard for sales performance monitoring

## Tech Stacks

- Microsoft Excel: For data cleaning, pivot tables and visualisation 
- Office Scripts: For KPI automation

## Dataset Description

The dataset includes: Date, Product Category, Product Name, Unit Price, Quantity, Revenue, CustomerID, Age, Location, Purchase History, and Branch.

## Insights

### 1. Sales Performance Overview
- **Total Revenue** reached **$32,089,767** with **749883** **Units sold.**
- **Gross Profit** amounted to **$25,682,552** resulting in a **profit margin** of **80.03.%**
- **YoY growth** stands at only **0.12%** despite strong profitability, suggesting very little revenue growth.

### 2. Products Performance
- Product category analysis revealed a relatively stable revenue across product categories, with **Make-up** slightly edging other products with the highest revenue generated **(6,461,096)** and **Units sold** **151,249.**
- All product categories have a similar **Profit Margin** of approximately **80%** with fragranc have the lowest margin of **79.98%**

### 3. Branch Performance
- Sales performance across branches is relatively balanced; however, **Branch A** is the highest-performing branch, generating approximately **$10,808,928** in revenue.
- Branches B and C follow closely behind, generating approximately **$10,622,037** and **$10,658,805** respectively.

### 4. Sales Trend Analysis
- Monthly sales trend reveals a steady increase in revenue from the beginning of the year, with sales peaking in **August**, and a gradual decline toward the end of the year.

### 5. Customer Demographics
- Revenue distribution by age group shows that middle-aged customers contribute the largest share of revenue, accounting for approximately **50%** of total sales.
- Young adults contribute **27.06%**, while older customers account for **22.88%** of total revenue.

### 6. Regional Analysis
- Contrary to expectations, rural areas outperform urban and suburban areas.

## Recommendations

### 1. Improve Revenue Growth
With year-over-year growth at only 0.12%, strategies should be implemented to stimulate demand.

### 2. Expand High-Performing Product Categories
Increase marketing investment and product variety in the Makeup category, as it generates the highest revenue and profitability.

### 3. Optimise Seasonal Sales
Increase marketing campaigns and inventory levels before peak sales periods, particularly during mid-year months when demand is highest.

### 4. Target Younger Customers
Develop marketing campaigns that appeal to young adult consumers, particularly through digital and social media platforms.
